Guide

N — displacement node: particle never moves; pressure and KE swing to maximum.

AN — displacement antinode: maximum particle motion; pressure is always zero here.

KE and PE are anti-phased in both space and time — when one peaks the other vanishes, but their sum stays constant on average.
